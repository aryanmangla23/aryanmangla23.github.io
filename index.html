// about

I am a neuroengineer investigating the circuit mechanisms of cognitive control. My work bridges computational psychiatry and systems neuroscience to decode how the OFC encodes value and filters noise. By integrating high-density recordings with reinforcement learning, I aim to develop translational models for neuromodulation to address psychiatric and neurological disorders.

// current research

Applying machine learning to pediatric scalp EEG and intracranial sEEG to identify biomarkers for Neurotrauma and model network entropy in Epilepsy.
